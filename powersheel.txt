环境变量：environment
1、查看环境变量
    a、查看所有环境变量
    get-childitem env:
    cd env: 
    ls
    b、查看用户环境变量
    [environment]::getEnvironmentVariable("Path", "user")
    c、查看系统环境变量
    [environment]::getEnvironmentVariable("path", "machine")

 2、删除环境变量
    [environment]::setEnvironmentVariable("Path", $null, "user")
    [environment]::setEnvironmentVariable("path", $null, "machine")

  3、设置环境变量
    [system.environment]::setEnvironmentVariable("my_var","value","user")
    [system.environment]::setEnvironmentVariable("my_var","value","machine")

 
 keyword: environment , variable, machine
